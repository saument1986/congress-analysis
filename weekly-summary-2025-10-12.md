## H.R.3123 - Ernest Peltz Accrued Veterans Benefits Act

**Chamber:** 🏛️ House
**Status:** Placed on the Union Calendar, Calendar No. 291
**Impact Score:** 4/10

### What It Does
- Allows VA pension payments due but unpaid at veteran's death to be paid to surviving family members
- Establishes order of beneficiaries: spouse, children, dependent parents, then estate
- Extends certain pension payment limits to December 31, 2031

### Financial Impact
- Pension payments to survivors of deceased veterans

### Who's Affected
**Benefits:** Surviving spouses of veterans, Children of deceased veterans, Dependent parents of deceased veterans
**Affected:** Veterans receiving VA pensions, Families of deceased veterans, Department of Veterans Affairs
